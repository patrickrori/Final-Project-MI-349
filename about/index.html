Patrick Rori

Growing up near Detroit, Michigan, automotive culture has been a part of my life since the very start.
Coming from a family of engineers, working on cars has been a large part of my life.
However, I took an interest into media in high school and started to photograph and make videos of cars around me.
Fast-forward a couple years, I've doubled downed on media and am attending college for such a career path.
Working as a media intern for a Michigan based racing company, I started Driveway Media as a means of getting money out of an unpaid internship.
Now, running a media company based on passion, I am able to create meaningful content for clients.
